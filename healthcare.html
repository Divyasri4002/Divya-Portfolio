AI-Powered Mobile Health Platform

Objective: To provide accessible healthcare delivery in rural areas by addressing challenges like limited resources, reliance on paper records, and the lack of centralized systems.

Key Features:

  • Integrated Platform: Web and mobile apps with an offline-first design using SQLite for local storage and Firebase for cloud synchronization.
  • IoT-Enabled Devices: Integration with health devices for real-time health monitoring.
  • AI-Powered Predictions: Leveraging TensorFlow Lite for on-device, real-time health predictions.
  • Chronic Condition Monitoring: Uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for continuous tracking of patient vitals.
  • End-to-End Encryption: Ensures secure handling of sensitive health information.
  • User-Centric Design: Optimized for usability in rural settings, with intuitive interfaces and support for low-bandwidth networks.

Tech Stack:

  • Frontend: React.js for web, Flutter for mobile
  • Backend: Node.js with Express, Firebase Cloud Functions
  • Database: SQLite (local), Firebase Realtime Database (cloud)
  • AI/ML: TensorFlow Lite

Impact:

  • Improved patient care with predictive insights.
  • Streamlined healthcare management for underserved regions.
  • Empowered healthcare workers with tools to make data-driven decisions.

Role:

  • Lead Developer - Working on integrating AI models and IoT devices for the mobile health platform.
  • Collaborator with a team of engineers for cloud infrastructure and data security.
